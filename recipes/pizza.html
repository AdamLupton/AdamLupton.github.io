Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 2 envelopes active dry yeast
  • 2 tsbp sugar
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 4 cups unbleached all-purpose flour

Preparation

Pour 1 1/2 cups warm water into a large bowl. Sprinkle with yeast and let stand until foamy; about 5 minutes.

Whisk sugar, oil, and salt into yeast mixture. Add flour and stir until a sticky dough forms. Transfer dough to an oiled bowl with plastic wrap covering and set aside in a warm, draft free place until the dough has doubled in size (about 1 hour).

Turn out onto lightly floured surface and gently knead 1 or 2 times before using. Split into 4 evenly sized chunks for small-medium sized pizzas.

[Optional] left over dough chunks can be frozen for up to 2 weeks and defrosted as needed to make pizza.
